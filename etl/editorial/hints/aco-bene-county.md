- This dataset reports the number of Medicare FFS beneficiaries assigned to each ACO, broken down by county of residence. It enables geographic visualization of ACO service areas and identifies which counties contribute the most beneficiaries to each ACO.
- Cell-size suppression applies: ACO-county combinations with fewer than 11 beneficiaries are suppressed. For ACOs with wide geographic spread (especially national ACOs), many counties will be suppressed, and summing the unsuppressed rows will undercount the ACO's total assigned beneficiaries.
- Beneficiary assignment reflects CMS's attribution methodology: beneficiaries are assigned to the ACO whose participant providers furnished the plurality of their primary care services. This is a retrospective calculation and may not align with the beneficiary's self-identified primary care relationship.
- The county is the beneficiary's county of residence, not the county where they received care. Beneficiaries who travel across county lines for care will be counted in their home county.
- This dataset uses a different performance year than the MSSP performance file in some releases (e.g., PY2023 beneficiary counts paired with PY2024 financial results). Temporal alignment should be verified.
