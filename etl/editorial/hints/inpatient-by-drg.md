- This dataset reports hospital-level discharge counts, average covered charges, average total payments, and average Medicare payments for each MS-DRG (Medicare Severity Diagnosis Related Group). It covers only IPPS (Inpatient Prospective Payment System) hospitals — critical access hospitals, Maryland waiver hospitals, and specialty hospitals are excluded.
- "Average Covered Charges" represent the hospital's billed charges, not what Medicare or patients actually paid. Charges are set by hospitals and vary enormously (often 3-5x actual costs). They should never be interpreted as prices — use "Average Total Payments" for a closer approximation of actual payment.
- Provider-DRG combinations with fewer than 11 discharges are excluded per CMS cell-size rules. This removes low-volume services (e.g., rare surgical DRGs at community hospitals) and systematically biases the dataset toward higher-volume provider-DRG pairs.
- DRG codes are revised annually by CMS. Comparing the same DRG code across years requires verifying that the clinical definition has not changed — MS-DRG reclassifications can shift patient populations between codes without any change in actual clinical practice.
- The dataset does not include outlier payments (cases where costs exceed a threshold and Medicare pays an additional amount). Hospitals with many outlier cases may have higher true average payments than this dataset indicates.
- Total payments include Medicare payments plus beneficiary cost-sharing (deductibles, coinsurance). They do not include supplemental insurance payments from Medigap or employer-sponsored plans.
